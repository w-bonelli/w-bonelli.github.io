About

I'm an IT professional trained in computer science and psychology, with 4+ years of experience developing software for research and enterprise. I'm presently most interested in the philosophy, psychology, and praxis of human ecology in the context of environmental crisis mitigation/adaptation. Other interests include:
  • Applied mathematics (category theory, linear control systems)
  • Data visualization
  • Linguistics/literature/semiotics
  • Philosophy of science
  • Social/cognitive psychology and cognitive science
I write things here sometimes. Usually I have no relevant qualifications to do so. Please don't hesitate to point out why I'm obviously mistaken and I'll do my best to fix it.
